Statesmen: Advisory Board

Chris Cannon
Advisory Board Purpose:
The advisory board will be comprised of people of clear, explicit political vision, paired generally as lefties and righties, who love America. This means that they love the institutions (like our banking system or the Uniform Commercial Code or religions competing in an environment of free speech), our foundation in principles embodied in the Constitution which, in turn, are built on the founding principles of the Declaration of Independence. We recognize the responsibility of governing in a changing world sometimes requires new laws or institutions and we are committed to finding solutions to our problems consistent with the concepts that have made America great.
Advisory Board Agreement:
If an advisory board member ever decides to leave the board, Statesmen PAC will post his reasons for at least five days on the front page of the StatesmenPAC website.
